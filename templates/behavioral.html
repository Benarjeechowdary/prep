Behavioural Round Interview Questions

Total Questions: 15

  • Can you describe a challenging situation at work and how you handled it?
    Provide a specific example using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
  • Tell me about a time when you had to work under pressure.
    Explain how you managed stress and successfully handled the situation.
  • How do you handle conflicts with coworkers?
    Describe a situation where you resolved a conflict through communication and compromise.
  • Give an example of a time when you showed leadership skills.
    Explain how you took charge of a situation and motivated others.
  • Tell me about a mistake you made at work and how you handled it.
    Focus on how you took responsibility and the steps you took to fix it.
  • Describe a time when you had to meet a tight deadline.
    Explain how you prioritized tasks and managed time to meet the deadline.
  • Have you ever had to deal with a difficult client or stakeholder?
    Describe how you handled the situation with professionalism and problem-solving skills.
  • How do you handle constructive criticism?
    Explain how you listen, learn, and apply feedback to improve.
  • Tell me about a time when you had to adapt to a significant change.
    Describe how you remained flexible and adjusted to the new situation.
  • What motivates you at work?
    Explain what drives you to perform well and stay committed to your goals.
  • Describe a time when you had to persuade someone to see things your way.
    Use the STAR method to explain how you presented your case and influenced the outcome.
  • Tell me about a time when you worked in a team to achieve a goal.
    Describe the role you played, how you collaborated, and the outcome.
  • How do you prioritize your work when you have multiple tasks to complete?
    Explain how you organize tasks, set deadlines, and manage your time.
  • Give an example of a time when you had to think on your feet.
    Explain how you assessed the situation quickly and made a decision.
  • How do you handle failure?
    Describe how you reflect on failures, learn from them, and improve.
